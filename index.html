Escape Room: The Locked Bedroom

You wake up in an unfamiliar bedroom, the air heavy with tension. The door is locked, and the only way out is to solve the mysteries hidden within the room. As you explore, old letters, faded papers, and forgotten clues seem to whisper secrets of those who came before you. The computer lock under the bed holds the final key to your freedom, but it’s locked away with no conventional keys in sight. The only way to unlock the door is to piece together the puzzles that were left behind. Time is running out—solve them before it’s too late.
